About Me

I am Kanayo-Egwuonwu Nnedi, a passionate and developing researcher in pure mathematics with a strong foundational background in algebra and symmetry. Currently transitioning from mathematics education into advanced mathematical research, with a specific interest in group theory, algebraic structures, and their applications in symmetry.

Motivated to pursue postgraduate studies (Master's/MRes) and contribute to mathematical theory, research-informed teaching, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

I hold a B.Sc(Ed) in Science Education (Mathematics) from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, and a teaching certificate from the Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria. With over four years of teaching experience, I bring both practical classroom expertise and a growing research interest in algebraic studies of group actions.

My research interest is focused on "An Algebraic Study of Group Actions and Their Applications in Symmetry." I am also skilled in abstract and analytical reasoning, mathematical writing, proof construction, and have basic familiarity with GAP software.
